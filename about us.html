COPD stands for Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease. It is a progressive lung disease that makes breathing tough due to narrowed airways. It includes chronic bronchitis, causing inflammation and excess mucus, and emphysema, which damages lung air sacs, reducing their elasticity.

Managing COPD involves diverse methods, including quitting smoking, medications, rehabilitation, and, in severe cases, advanced treatments like oxygen therapy or surgery and those suffering from COPD benefit immensely from early detection and a customized approach to therapy.
